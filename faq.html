General questions

What platforms will pixus run on?

Pixus will be initially released on iOS. This will be followed by a website and then Android.

How is pixus different?

Current image sharing apps send your photos to a central server which then figures out what other people want to see and sends it to their devices. With pixus images spread organically. You post an image and it gets sent to the people connected to you, as people like the image it spreads via their connections.

How does it work?

Pixus uses a number of technologies to allow devices to communicate. Depending on circumstances pixus may connect with WebSockets, WebRTC or Multipeer.
The user of course doesn't have to worry about this.

Does it work offline?

On iOS pixus uses multipeer to connect so if nearby people are using the app you can browse each others collections without access to the Internet.

Where can I get pixus?

Pixus is still under heavy development and is not yet available
