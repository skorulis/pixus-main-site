A revolution in photo sharing

It's all about the journey

As you view photos your interactions take you on a photo journey. There's no destination, just more customised content for you to view.

Location and time centric

Being able to see images isn't enough. Sometimes you want to know what's happening in specific location at a specific time

Forming connections

As you view images pixus learns what you like and finds users who have similar interests.
